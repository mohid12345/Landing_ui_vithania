Driven by a deep-rooted curiosity, he pursued organic chemistry during his college years, studying the phytoconstituents of herbs such as Ashwagandha. This foundation laid the groundwork for his later integration of Ayurvedic principles with modern scientific research. Later, he explored the intersection of Ayurveda and modern science, leading to pioneering research in diabetic medicines, earning him a PhD from Anna University and shaping his vision for Vithania.

🌿 Origin of the Brand Name
VITHANIA

As a curious young boy, Rajesh (Dr. A. Rajesh Kumar) watched his grandfather advise a ‘vaidyar’ to boil Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) in milk. When he asked why, his grandfather smiled and said, “Ashwagandha holds both fat-soluble and water-soluble constituents. Milk unlocks them all.”

That moment sparked a lifelong fascination. Rajesh pursued organic chemistry, uncovering the science behind phytoconstituents and Ayurveda’s wisdom. The first herb that captivated him was Ashwagandha– nature’s powerhouse, just as his grandfather had said.

Years later, in honour of that revelation, Vithania was born — a name rooted in tradition, validated by science.

This tribute celebrates the revelation that ignited a lifelong pursuit of innovation and discovery, reflecting our commitment to the ancient principles of Ayurvedic wellness.
